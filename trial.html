About

A New Era for Guwahati Airport

Guwahati, the vibrant capital of Assam and gateway to India's Northeast, is poised for a transformative leap with the development of a new integrated terminal at Lokpriya Gopinath Bordoloi International Airport (LGBI). Inspired by the natural beauty of Assam and the intricate elegance of Japanese origami, the terminal's design combines functionality with cultural and environmental harmony. Set to handle 13.1 million passengers annually by 2030, this state-of-the-art facility promises a seamless travel experience with spacious interiors, advanced baggage handling systems, modern security checkpoints, diverse retail and dining options, and eco-friendly construction.
